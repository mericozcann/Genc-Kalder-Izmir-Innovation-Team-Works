## Overview
The **GençKalDer İzmir Innovation Team** focuses on developing innovative, technical, entrepreneurial and creative solutions.  
This repository contains the team’s work, idea development processes, project documents and meeting notes.

---

## Mission
To create an innovation-oriented, collaborative environment where young individuals develop impactful solutions that contribute to organizational development, personal growth and the broader community.

## Working Principles

- **Collaboration:** Members participate actively and share responsibilities.  
- **Transparency:** All processes and decisions are clearly communicated.  
- **Innovation:** Every member contributes at least one idea or improvement.  
- **Accountability:** Tasks are completed on time.  
- **Growth:** Members commit to continuous personal betterment.  

---

## Meeting Structure

### General Meetings
Held after exam periods or major organizational milestones.

### Team Meetings
Conducted **every two weeks**, online or in person, focusing on:
- Idea development  
- Task distribution  
- Project planning  
- Progress evaluation  

---

## Upcoming Tasks (Until the Next Meeting)
