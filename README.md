## Overview
The **GençKalDer İzmir Innovation Team** focuses on developing innovative, technical, entrepreneurial and creative solutions.  
This repository documents the team’s work, idea development processes, project documents and meeting notes.

---

## Mission
To create an innovation-oriented, collaborative environment where young individuals develop impactful solutions that contribute to organizational development, personal growth and the broader community.

## Genel Bakış
**GençKalDer İzmir İnovasyon Takımı**, teknik, yaratıcı ve girişimcilik odaklı çözümler geliştirmeyi amaçlayan bir çalışma ekibidir.
